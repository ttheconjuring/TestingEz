Frequently Asked Questions

"TestingEz" is a place where everyone is free to create and join tests on different topics.😁 It aims to simultaneously educate and entertain people, since there is no limits on what tests or questions you are trying to create. It was created as an alternative to some way more famous testing platforms as: Google Forms, Kahoot, Typeform, FlexiQuiz and others.

In the beginning, "TestingEz" was actually a desktop application💻 devised and made for school diploma project. It had very limited options compared to the current version of the project, but was good enough to earn me an excellent grade. Although I knew the realization was far from great, the idea was definitely a good one and eventually grew into web application🌐. To cut long story short, this is how "TestingEz" came to existence.

The "We..." messages you might have encountered while discovering the potential of "TestingEz" (hopefully not 😅) refers only to "Me". Believe it or not, the developer team of this "amazing" project was consisted only of one person - unfortunately me.

Absolutely, not. The initial budget of "TestingEz" was exactly 0💲 (or slightly beneath it) and that's why it is totally free.

While "TestingEz" is generally safe, we can't guarantee that you won't come upon some disturbing, strange, threatening tests or questions.

So, have fun and be warned!
